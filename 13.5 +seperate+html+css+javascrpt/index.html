It’s Time to Move Forward

Deuteronomy 1:6

"The Lord our God spake unto us in Horeb, saying, Ye have dwelt long enough in this mount." (Deuteronomy 1:6)

I. God Sees When You’ve Stayed Too Long

God notices when you've stayed too long in one place. He is calling you higher!

  • Psalm 33:13-14 - God looks from heaven and sees all mankind.
  • Reflection: Where have you been circling too long?

II. Reasons People Camp Too Long

  • Fear of the unknown.
  • Comfort and complacency.
  • Disobedience or doubt.

III. God’s Call to Advancement

  • Deuteronomy 1:7 - Turn and take your journey.
  • Isaiah 43:19 - God is doing a new thing.
  • Joshua 1:3 - Every place you step, He gives to you.

IV. How to Respond to God’s Call

  • Recognize the time (Ecclesiastes 3:1).
  • Renew your mind (Romans 12:2).
  • Rely on God’s promise (Deuteronomy 31:8).

V. Closing Prayer

“Lord, I hear Your voice today. I will not stay where You’ve called me out of. Give me courage to step into the new season You have prepared for me. I am moving forward, in Jesus' name. Amen.”
